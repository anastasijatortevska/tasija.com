About

I competed in international math olympiads through high school — IMO, EGMO (Bronze), Balkan, Mediterranean, and others. I studied CS and mathematics at Columbia, where I spent two years as a TA for Discrete Mathematics and CS Theory across classes of 300 students. I'm now at ETH Zürich for a master's specializing in theory.

I also have a few years of industry experience as a software engineer, mostly backend and full-stack work. I find it useful to know both sides.

In my personal life I'm into art, video games, and martial arts. I hold a 1st dan in Goju-ryu karate and 9th kyu in Shotokan karate :)

Research Interests
