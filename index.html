About

MS CS at ETH Zürich, specializing in theory. Before that, CS and math at Columbia — two years TA'ing Discrete Mathematics and CS Theory across 300-student classes, international math olympiads through high school (IMO, EGMO Bronze, Balkan, Mediterranean), and a few years of industry SWE experience.

In my personal life I'm into art, video games, and martial arts. I hold a 1st dan in Goju-ryu karate and 9th kyu in Shotokan karate :)

Research Interests

Broadly in discrete algorithms and complexity — I'm most interested in when combinatorial structure makes hard problems tractable, and how well we can do.

  • Approximation algorithms
  • Graph algorithms & graph theory
  • Computational complexity
  • Parameterized complexity
  • Combinatorics

Projects

Way2Play

Platform for finding accessible courts, playing fields, and sport communities in NYC. Ruby on Rails with Google Maps API for real-time local court data.
